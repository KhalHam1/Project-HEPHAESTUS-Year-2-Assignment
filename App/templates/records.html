RECORD OF HEROES

Return Home
"Remember those who dedicate their lives to a cause bigger than the Earth, but also those who do little things with a heart wider than the sun."-Hamid

These great heroes that have embraced project HEPHAESTUS have proven that impossibilites are the specks of a bygone age.

  1. Arxideya






    • Occupation: Ranger

    • 6700 Lost Rescued

    • 312 Hephaestian Missions Completed

    • Felled the Wayward Star to protect the Galactica

    • Provided aid to the wounded after the Great Shatter

    • Weided a powerful bow and fired a variety of damaging arrows
      •




  2. Cremus Povar




    • Occupation: Medic

    • 200 Lost Rescued

    • 314 Hephaestian Missions Completed

    • Merged his DNA with the DNA of 60 crop producing plant species.

    • Saved 200 villages from famine and death by simultanoeusly growing a variety of crops across the Galactica and inhaling the poisonous gas after a large gas explosion.

    • Found Master Mo Ni Kai after a battle in 531 BC and nursed him back to health, returning him to the Galactica along with 166 other Lost.



  3. Captain Narkitange Changa




    • Occupation: Ship Captain and Entrepreneur

    • Freed 600 slaves from the Isla de Bandida Prison so that they could escape the Great Shatter.

    • Sailed 700 Africans, 368 Malagasies and 71 Egyptians to Cape Town Port just in time to board last ship to the Galactica before the Great Shatter.

    • Was the lead organizer and sponsor for the first football world cup on the Galactica

    • Donated his organs to save his mutilated son Akiibi who then joined Project Hephaestus to follow in his father's footsteps.

    • Ship was sunk by followers of Kobra'Al. No members of the crew were ever found.

    • It is believed that he still lives, but how could it be possible?
      •



  4. Llian Aquarius




    • Occupation: Unknown

    • 6 000 000 Lost Rescued.

    • Provided a safe haven under the ocean for thousands during the great shatter.

    • Saved all of humanity by defeating the tyrannical Commander Kobra'Al before he could launch 1200 nuclear missiles from his hideout in the Great Ocean.

    • Her Waterbreathing elixir has provided humanity with the possibility of breathing underwater for a limited time.



  5. Logan Legend

    • Occupation: Protector of the Realm.

    • 98 000 000 Lost Rescued.

    • The first Hephaestian.

    • Outcast by the Great Shatter, he returned to the present day by his own accord, a hero of many civilizations.

    • Traversed multiple galaxies, gaining the aid of 111 different species that helped us construct the Galactica, our new home after the Solar System's destruction.

    • Having mastered 840 alien languages, he led humanity into the unknown by teaching us the foreign ways

    • Bearing a celestial map to a chain of new habitable worlds etched into his mind, he will lead humanity into a bright future.
